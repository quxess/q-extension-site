Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following Chrome extensions developed by Q Extension:

SnapShot Tab Volume Save As JPG/PNG

What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • Personal identification information
  • Payment information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

License Key Validation (Tab Volume)

Tab Volume offers optional premium features unlocked via a one-time license key purchase. If you purchase a premium license, the license key you enter is sent to Lemon Squeezy (our payment and licensing provider) solely to validate your purchase. This validation happens once on activation and periodically in the background to confirm the license remains valid.

We do not store or have access to your payment details. All payment processing is handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy. For information on how Lemon Squeezy handles your data, please refer to their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

SnapShot and Save As JPG/PNG are currently fully free and do not involve any license key validation or communication with external services.

Data Stored Locally

All data stored by these extensions remains on your device only and is never transmitted to us.

SnapShot
  • Screenshot history (images stored on your device only)
  • User preferences (filename pattern, image format, theme)
Tab Volume
  • Per-tab volume and mute settings (premium users only)
  • License key and premium status
Save As JPG/PNG
  • User preferences (output format, JPEG quality setting)

Third Party Services

Tab Volume only interacts with the following third party service:

Lemon Squeezy (lemonsqueezy.com)
  • Purpose: License key activation and validation
  • Data sent: License key only
  • Privacy policy: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

SnapShot and Save As JPG/PNG do not communicate with any external services. All processing happens locally on your device.

Permissions

Each extension requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality. Detailed permission justifications are provided on each extension's Chrome Web Store listing.

Changes to This Policy

When SnapShot and Save As JPG/PNG introduce premium features in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. The updated version will always be posted at this URL with a revised date.
